Like lifting but need to get your form and endurance up?

Do these:

!PLEASE WORK WITH WEIGHTS YOU ARE COMFORTABLE WITH!

Bench press: 3 sets 10 reps each at 70% body weight (1 minute break in between sets)
1 minute and 30 seconds breaks
Rows (do pull ups if no rowing machine): 3 sets of 10 reps each at 60% body weight (1 minute break in between sets)
1 minute and 30 seconds breaks
Bicep curls: Using a 25 lb barbell do 7 reps going half way to the top, 7 reps starting from half and going full, 7 reps doing full reps (no breaks in between)
1 minute and 30 seconds breaks
Legs: 3 sets 10 reps each at 100% body weight (2 minute breaks in between sets)
1 minute and 30 seconds breaks
Tricep extension: 3 sets 10 reps each at 30 lb barbell (or whatever you are comfortable with)
