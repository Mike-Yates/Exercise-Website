Like lifting but need to get your form and endurance up?

Do these:

!PLEASE WORK WITH WEIGHTS YOU ARE COMFORTABLE WITH!

Bench press : 3 sets 10 reps each at 70% body weight
1 minute and 30 seconds breaks
Rows : 3 sets of 10 reps each at 60% body weight
1 minute and 30 seconds breaks
Bicep curls : Do 21's using a 25 lb barbell
1 minute and 30 seconds breaks
Squats: 3 sets 10 reps each at 100% body weight
1 minute and 30 seconds breaks
Tricep extension : 3 sets 10 reps each at 30 lb barbell
